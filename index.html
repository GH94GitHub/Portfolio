Tony Henderson

Hello and welcome to my portfolio, my name is Tony and I'm a Full Stack Developer living in Nebraska. I find tremendous pleasure in learning and applying new skills and just overall improvement from iteration to the next. I enjoy delving in and perfecting new frameworks and libraries as well as the most efficient/performant ways to handle web solutions.

When I'm not working on a project my happiness comes from watching my little girl explore everything this world has to offer. Away from the computer I keep my mind engaged with 3D puzzles and finding the right solution to web challenges brings me much of the same satisfaction, the bigger the hill is in front of you, the better the view is standing at the top. At the end of the day providing value through automated process or just developing beautiful views, the web has something to offer everyone, both business and consumer alike and I thoroughly enjoy being a part in bridging that gap.
